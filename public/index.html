About Giftowave

Giftowave is a premium gifting studio born at IIT Kharagpur, built on the philosophy that every memory deserves a spotlight. We craft bespoke, high-fashion magazines where your personal milestones become cover stories. From our signature 'Vogue Edition' to nostalgia-infused 'F.R.I.E.N.D.S' issues, we combine minimalist design with luxury materials to deliver a gift that feels as exclusive as a high-end publication.

Privacy & Safety

At Giftowave, your media is sacred. Your photographs and details are used strictly for the custom design process. We utilise secure cloud infrastructure and never share your data with external parties. Once your premium magazine is delivered, your data is securely archived. For payments, we use Razorpay—India's leading secure payment gateway—ensuring your financial transactions are encrypted and private.
